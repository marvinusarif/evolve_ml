Seminar dan Workshop Medis: Clinical Management Update in Pediatric Emergencies (CUPE) 2012, 31 Maret-1 April 2012, FKUI Jakarta
Departemen Ilmu Kesehatan Anak FKUI-RSCM bekerja sama dgn PLD FKUI 2012 mempersembahkan seminar &amp; workshop "Clinical Management Update in Pediatric Emergencies (CUPE) 2012" pada 31 Mar-1 Apr 2012.

Topik seminar:
1. Neonatal Stabilizer
2. Pediatric Basic Life Support
3. Asthma &amp; Respiratory Failure in Children
4. Febrile Seizure
5. Management of Altered Mental State in Children
6. Malnutrition Emergency
7. Clinical Approach to Diarrhea &amp; Dehydration in Children
8. Identification and Management of Shock in Children
9. Fluid and Electrolyte Therapy in Children

Topik workshop:
1. Basic Life Support
2. Pediatric Intubation
3. Intravenous and Intraosseus Access
4. Fluid and Electrolyte Imbalance: Diagnostic &amp; Therapy
5. Altered States of Conciousness: Diagnostic &amp; Therapy
6. Radiology in Pediatric Emergency: Lecture &amp; Interpretating
